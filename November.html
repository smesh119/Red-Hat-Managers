MANAGER MEETING RECAP: Engaging associates using Red Hat's rewards portfolio

Leaders from around the organization discussed variable compensation as part of the Total Rewards portfolio.

  • Kent Pritchett, Director of Compensation and Benefits, discussed updates to the Total Rewards strategy, including the new salary grade structures and manager dashboards, as well as the importance of communication in the bonus cycle.
  • Frank Calderoni, EVP Operations and CFO, explained how the pool is funded and how managers can help associates better understand their payouts.
  • Marco Bill-Peter, VP Customer Experience and Engagement and BJ Walker, Manager Platform Product Support, discussed how they structure and manage variable compensation in their organization, as well as their best practices in distributing and communicating bonuses.

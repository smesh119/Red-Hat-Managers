UPPING OUR GAME: LET'S GET STUFF DONE TOGETHER

Frank Calderoni, EVP, Operations + CFO, gave some background on who he is and why he chose Red Hat, described his vision for how we can “up our game,” and answered your top questions.

About Frank
  • Started as an intern at IBM and worked there for 21 years
  • Left IBM and relocated to California to work as SVP and CFO at SanDisk for 2+ years, then worked at QLogic for 2+ years as SVP and CFO
  • Left QLogic and became EVP and CFO at Cisco for 11 years, then relocated to Raleigh and is now EVP, Operations and CFO at Red Hat
How do we up our game?
  • Grow revenue and increase growth in subscriptions and customers
  • Accelerate and up-sell emerging technology products and solutions
  • Continue to drive value and positive reputation in the marketplace
Your top questions:
  • What are our biggest challenges and opportunities?
    • Accelerating our revenue growth
    • Building our capabilities to scale within the company and planning and forecasting our hiring needs
  • What is your leadership style and what culture do you want to promote?
    • Debate, then commit. Have authentic and honest discussions before making decisions
    • Know what's expected and what success looks like, and know how it aligns to the company's mission
  • What is your perspective on talent development?
    • Develop people and give them opportunities to experience new things and develop their skills

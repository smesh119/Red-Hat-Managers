CULTURE + LEADERSHIP STRATEGY

Mark Rumbles, Principal Architect, People team

Mark talked about the role of our company values in scaling Red Hat’s culture, including what things we do that drive our culture and what detracts from it. He also talked about two key skills necessary for managing with our values:

  • Spotting situations where our values of freedom, courage, commitment, and accountability are not being used in balance
  • Being able to coach associates to recognize and correct the imbalance so they can maximize relationships and results
PANEL DISCUSSION: MANAGING WITH OUR VALUES

Moderated by Mark Rumbles, featuring

  • Matt Hicks, VP, Engineering, OpenShift and Management
  • Susan Buchanan, Senior Manager, Inside Sales
  • John Chapman, Manager, IT
  • Matt Parson, Treasurer

Panelists shared different ideas on how they help their associates live Red Hat’s values in balance, as well as how they use these values to tackle important and sometimes challenging issues.
